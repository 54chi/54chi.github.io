About 54chi

01 January 2016 on blog

In 2016, Sachi decided to take a leap year (aka: quit his job). Tired with the status quo of the IT consulting world (some examples here), he is planning to rethink the way enterprise IT should operate in the modern world, while at the same time doing some upgrading to his skills and travel around the World to prevent this.

At least that's the theory. Sachi is addicted to coffee shops, and decent internet speed...and while he planned a small budget to do this crazy plan, it is not going to last forever...unless he finds gold in one of his expeditions (and/or is self-financed by one if his projects ;))

Sachi is not a professional blogger, and this site is most likely not going to be promoted outside of his circle of friends. The main purpose of 54chi.com is to serve as a log for this year long Journey, and what happens by the end of it (and show off what I can do by myself).
