And...we are (a)live!

31 December 2015 on blog

Welcome to the new year.

This will be my new and most eclectic blog of them all, so I hope it won't get too confusing. 2016 is the year when I'll be making one of the biggest challenges in my life: travel abroad for an extended period of time.

Sure, for many of you this is nothing too complicated, but here is the difference: by traveling, I expect to get inspired and work on something cool, as in "get the freaking idea, and put it to work" cool.

A while ago, I discovered this site: http://50problems50days.com/, and wondered if I can do something similar but with code.

The plan is to create something over a short period of time (e.g. 2~3 months), make a release, and move on with the next idea (country?).

I am not sure how far I can get with my current savings (and passport), but time will tell.

Wish you a Happy New Year.
