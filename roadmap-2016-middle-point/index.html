Roadmap 2016 - Middle point

02 July 2016 on blog

When I started 2016, I created a wishlist of "things" that I would like to do. Call it New Year's resolutions, call it a Plan, a Schedule, Roadmap or whatever.

I've tried to stick with the schedule as best as I could, but there have been many changes.

Let's do a quick reality check.

Trips:

  • Trips done: Peru (South America), Canada and USA (North America)
  • Trips added: Costa Rica, Peru (again)
  • Trips not done (yet): (Thailand) SE Asia, Australia, other-than-Peru South America

What happened:

  • My friends in Thailand told me is better to go around December, cause is less hot.
  • I'm still considering doing a "round-the-world" trip, but not sure if I'll get the time to do so.

Research:

  • Research done: Data analytics for automatic trading (Investments research); AgilePM, SAFe and Spotify PM (Project Management Research); Spotify, Twitter and Facebook architectures (High Scalability Architectures); Phoenix+Elixir; Neo4j (Graph Databases); Paypal, Stripe (Payment Gateways); Estonia, UK (Government as a Platform); Supply Chain and Logistics research; Manufacturing research
  • Research added: ArangoDB, OrientDB (Graph Databases); GraphQL; OTP; Serverless; Conversational UI/UX; Elm; AWS Lambda; Firebase; React+Mobx; React+Redux; Bot frameworks
  • Research not done (yet): Sail.js, Kraken.js, Enterprise communication tools; Polymer; rethinkDB; AI research; SEO and Analytics; Porting web applications (this one is discarded)

What happened:

  • I learned about Serverless while doing some DevOps and wanted to maximize my AWS and Azure cloud servers/services. This delayed the whole plan to use Phoenix+Elixir until there is a better way to do it under Serverless (but if push comes to shove, I still have my cloud9 instance)
  • I'll probably won't do any Sail, Kraken or Polymer. I'd rather do more Elm in the future
  • Elm and Elixir made me want to do more things "the functional way". In javascript, this is kinda the approach of Redux and Mobx, hence the inclusion

Projects:

  • Projects done (shipped): 54chi.com (CMS Project); CSS Comics (HTML5); Vagrant+docker and Deis (Devops); Autotrader (under the Zorro platform); OriconCast (NodeJS); TapToTipToTheTippingTag (IoT/RFID)
  • Projects added: Serverless for Alexa (Devops); Resume App (HTML5); Hobbo Explorer (IoT/Alexa Skill); Buenos Aires Metro GTFS (Graph for Transportation); Chatbot
  • Projects not done (yet): Agile Enterprise Tool; Graph CRM; Enterprise Collaboration Tool; Trading Card Game; Idea to Market; Supply Chain

What happened:

  • The support to host Phoenix and Elm apps in the cloud have not been stellar. I mean, I can host the apps in something like Cloud9 or my own servers, but they are not exactly "devops" friendly. Many things that work in Cloud9 won't work in Windows machines because of stupid OS
  • I made the mistake of signing for "windows insider" program, and now not even my Vagrant works. I don't want to depend on Cloud9 for my dev (not yet) or spend too much time trying to fix the local machine
  • And of course, my own "newbieness" in many of the things that I want to accomplish.

Of course, I still have until December, and as long as I don't create "new projects" along the way, I should be ok. But I know myself, and I know that I will most likely create a few, hehe.
